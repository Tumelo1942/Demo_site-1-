About Us

Mnotho Foundation is a social work-based organization dedicated to supporting individuals, families, and communities. Our mission is to provide compassionate counseling, social welfare guidance, and empowerment programs to promote well-being and social justice.

Our Services

Counseling & Therapy

Offering individual and group counseling to help clients navigate personal challenges with care and confidentiality.

Community Programs

Facilitating community-based projects aimed at education, empowerment, and poverty reduction.

Support Services

Providing referrals, advocacy, and emotional support to those in need of social assistance.
